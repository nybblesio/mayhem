Sound FX Truetype Dingbat Font for Windows

2003 Iconian Fonts - Daniel Zadorozny

http://www.iconian.com

This is a dingbats font of over 75 comic book sound effects and some word baloons.  Use of a character map program, such as Windows' Character Map, will be very helpful in exploring and using this font.
